Wilensky's Diner

hasn't changed much since it opened in 1932. It is still owned by the Wilensky family. It still has much of the original decor and even has some customers who date back to our first location. Many of them still remember Moe Wilensky, his wife Ruth and their son Bernard who worked hard for many years to make Wilensky's what it is today.
